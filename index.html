About


Hello! I am Fariha Raihan, a first-year student at the University of Technology Sydney, currently studying Information Technology. Originally from Bangladesh, I came to Australia as an international student to pursue my academic and personal goals. Alongside my studies, I’m also a professional henna artist with a passion for painting. I find joy in expressing creativity through art and music—two of my favorite hobbies.

Skills


I am really passionate about event management and have had the chance to organise school events and even my own high school prom! I learned how to bring creative ideas to life. I have always loved the idea of taking initiative and enjoyed the process of being backstage and seeing how the planning comes to life. Some of the key events I hosted back in school were the German Cultural Show, MUN Conference, Pohela Boishakh, etc.


Hobbies & Interests


I love painting and have always tried to explore my creative space. Didn't want to keep it limited to one thing only, so I tried different art forms which helped me discover my passion for henna art. I loved the intricacy and the way it worked, which eventually led me to start my own business that I have been running for 3 years! Apart from these, I enjoy photography and music, and I've attached a glimpse below of all my interests!
